============================================================
   CANDLE QUEST ACADEMY  -  How to start the game
============================================================

This is a game. No installing. Nothing to buy. It runs right
in your web browser, and it uses pretend "Koins" only -- never
real money.


------------------------------------------------------------
 THE QUICKEST WAY (works on any computer)
------------------------------------------------------------
   Just double-click:   index.html
   The game opens in your web browser. That's it!


------------------------------------------------------------
 PUT A CLICKABLE ICON ON THE DESKTOP (recommended for kids)
------------------------------------------------------------
 WINDOWS:
   1. Double-click:   Make Desktop Icon (Windows).bat
   2. A "Candle Quest" icon appears on the Desktop.
   3. Your son double-clicks that icon anytime to play.

 MAC:
   1. Double-click:   Make Desktop Icon (Mac).command
      (First time, Mac may say it's from an unidentified
       developer -- right-click the file, choose "Open",
       then click "Open" to allow it.)
   2. A "Candle Quest" icon appears on the Desktop.
   3. Your son double-clicks that icon anytime to play.

 Prefer not to make a desktop icon? You can also just
 double-click:
   Windows:  Play Candle Quest (Windows).bat
   Mac:      Play Candle Quest (Mac).command   (or Candle Quest.app)


------------------------------------------------------------
 NOTES FOR PARENTS
------------------------------------------------------------
 * Everything is simulated -- no sign-ups, no real trading,
   no internet needed after you have this folder.
 * Progress (XP, badges, rank) saves automatically in the
   browser on this computer.
 * Keep this whole folder together. The desktop icon points
   back to it, so don't delete or move the folder after
   making the icon (if you move it, just run the
   "Make Desktop Icon" step again).

 Have fun, and welcome to the dojo!  ⛩️
